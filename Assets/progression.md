# Combat Progression & Reward System

## Core System: Hybrid Rogue-Lite Campaign

The "Atomic Chain" combines permanent synthesis progression with replayable combat loops:

- **Campaign**: 118 nodes (one per element) with handcrafted/procedural challenges
- **Run Structure**: Use synthesized elements to push through waves, earn Binding Energy
- **Permanent Progress**: Keep elements on death; only lose run-specific resources
- **Endless Mode**: Unlocked after element 118 for leaderboard competition

### Progression Systems Comparison

| System | Pros | Cons | Best For |
|--------|------|------|----------|
| 50-Level Campaign | High polish, clear ending | High dev cost, limited replayability | Story-heavy RPGs |
| Pure Endless | Infinite content | Feels aimless/grindy | Hyper-casual games |
| Rogue-Lite | High replayability | Difficult balance | Skill-based action |
| **Hybrid (Recommended)** | **Permanent growth + Replayable loops** | **Complex to implement** | **Collection games** |

## Fatigue System (Atomic-Weight Based)

Prevents early-game stagnation while maintaining strategic depth:

**Formula**: `Fatigue (min) = Base + (Z × Multiplier)`

- **Early Game** (H, He, Li): 2–5 minutes (checked between runs)
- **Mid Game** (Fe, Au): 30–60 minutes (encourages team rotation)
- **Late Game** (U, Og): 90+ minutes (high-stakes decisions)

### Safety Nets
- **Early Collection Buff**: -75% fatigue if you own <10 elements
- **Perfect Synthesis Bonus**: 100% Stability = halved fatigue
- **Neutron Dust Shop**: Buy fatigue reduction or quick-clear consumables
